Project Overview:

**BusMate** is a " smart university transport service application " aimed at enhancing students' commuting experience through advanced features such as "trip booking, bus tracking, and instant notifications". The system provides a "comprehensive transport management solution" that integrates students, drivers, university administration, and service providers, thereby "optimizing and improving university transport efficiency".

BusMate - Smart University Transport System


BusMate** is a comprehensive system that provides smart solutions for university transport management, allowing students to **subscribe to the service, book trips, and track buses** effortlessly. It also enables drivers to **verify student subscriptions via QR Code** and allows university administration to **efficiently manage student data, trips, and bus stops**.
Backend Integration with BusMate :
- Developed **APIs** to facilitate **trip data exchange, subscription management, and real-time bus tracking**.
- Used **WebSockets & Event-Driven Architecture** to ensure **instant location updates and notifications**.
- Implemented **Token-Based Authentication & Role-Based Access Control** to ensure **data security and user permissions management**.



Challenges & Solutions


- **Real-time location tracking integration:** Implemented **WebSockets & Google Maps API** to ensure **accurate bus tracking**.
- **Performance optimization:** Applied **Redis Caching & Database Indexing** to accelerate queries and enhance response times.
- **Data security:** Implemented **JWT Authentication & Role-Based Access Control** to prevent unauthorized access.
